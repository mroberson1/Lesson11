Featured Projects

Cooper River Bridge Run

The Cooper River Bridge Run held every year in Charleston, SC is attended by over 40,000 runners. The run coordinators wanted colorful and eye-catching pieces that reflect the vibrancy of the city.

Team Flourish

Team Flourish is an in-house cosmetics sales team under BeautyCounter. BeautyCounter is all about using clean and natural ingredients, and they wanted their logo and designs to reflect that aesthetic.

Puffin House Publishing

Puffin House Publishers wanted to revamp the cover designs of some of their most famous childrens books. A simple, minimalistic design was created showcasing the characters of Roald Dahl’s beloved novels.
